When coming up with a design, I and my team member looked at a several of well-design websites and what they had common. The first thing we found that makes a good web design is that it has a purpose. Each page should be relevant and clear so users can take the information easy and find it engaging. This can be done by adding headings, subheadings, keywords etc. Also another thing that we had to consider is a professional typeface that is easy to read such as Arial or Verdana. What makes the website look good is a consistent colour theme and the use of images as not including this makes the site look bland and simple when the user does navigate through. All the basics of what makes a website was included in our designs as it’s a must have or it won’t look like a website e.g. footer, header, tabs. At the end of taking all the research and including the requirements, we created two designs and we both had our own basic designs to follow. When coding the webpages together and inputting elements and styling it in CSS, I did have to change some of our ideas which led me to create this design that you’re viewing.
